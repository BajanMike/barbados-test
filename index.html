Marine Life

Barbados is home to diverse marine ecosystems, including coral reefs, sea turtles, and tropical fish species. The waters around the island support hawksbill and leatherback turtles, which nest on our beaches.

Flora

The island's plant life includes mahogany trees, frangipani, and various species of palms. The Barbados National Flower, the Pride of Barbados (Caesalpinia pulcherrima), can be found throughout the island.

Fauna

Barbados wildlife includes the Green Monkey, introduced from West Africa, the Barbados Leaf-Toed Gecko, and numerous bird species like the Barbados Bullfinch.
